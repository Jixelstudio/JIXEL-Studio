Devlog #1 – The Journey Begins

Updates

January 10, 2025

Today marks the beginning of an exciting journey—I’m officially starting work on my very first PC game, "SLIMO"!

As an aspiring indie game developer, I’ve always dreamed of creating games from scratch, learning everything along the way. And today, that dream starts to take shape.

SLIMO will be my first project, a game made entirely by me, with no shortcuts—just creativity, determination, and a lot of learning. I know there will be challenges, bugs, and moments of frustration, but I’m ready to embrace every step.

This is the start of a journey into game development, and I can’t wait to share updates, progress, and lessons learned along the way.
